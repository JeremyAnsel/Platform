Platform.Xwa Namespace

The Platform.Xwa namespace provides types to manipulate XWA mission files

Classes

NameDescription
BriefingBriefing object for XWA
BriefingCollectionObject to hold all Briefings
FlightGroupObject for individual FlightGroups
FlightGroupCollectionObject to maintain mission FG list
GlobalsObject for individual Team's mission-wide goals
GlobalsCollectionObject to maintain all Team Global Goals
MessageObject for individual in-flight messages
MessageCollectionObject to maintain mission in-flight messages
MissionFramework for XWA
StringsObject for string lists used in XWA
TeamObject for individual Team settings
TeamCollectionObject to maintain Teams
